Our Story

Building Malaysia's Digital Future

Marz Technology & Trading began its journey in 2004 as a computer supply and repair business, serving clients in Gombak, Kuala Lumpur. Founded on principles of quality, integrity, and customer focus, we quickly earned a reputation for reliable service and technical expertise.

Over two decades, we have grown exponentially — expanding from computer hardware into comprehensive IT infrastructure, networking, cybersecurity, software development, and most recently, cutting-edge AI solutions. Today, we serve over 2,000 clients across government agencies, GLCs, private enterprises, and educational institutions throughout Malaysia.

Our team of certified professionals brings together expertise across multiple domains — from network engineering and system administration to artificial intelligence and software development. We are committed to continuous learning and innovation, ensuring our clients always benefit from the latest technology advancements.
